# Tournament Format Details

## Group Stage Structure
- Teams are divided into groups.
- Each team plays every other team in their group.
- Points are awarded for wins and draws.

## Playoff Bracket
- Top teams from each group advance to the playoffs.
- The playoffs consist of knockout rounds leading to the final.

## Seeding Policy
- Teams are seeded based on their performance in the previous tournaments.
- Higher seeds are given advantages in the initial rounds.

## VRS Information
- The VRS (Virtual Ranking System) is used for ranking teams based on their performance.
- VRS will impact seeding for future tournaments.
