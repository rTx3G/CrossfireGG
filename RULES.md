## 3. Scheduling

- All match times are **first-come, first-served**.
- All times are displayed in **EST/EDT**.
- **Map vetoes** occur immediately before matches.
- An admin may set a **default match time** if teams cannot agree on one.
- If a veto cannot be completed, a **random map or maps** will be assigned and cannot be changed.
- After a CS2 update, ISP outage, or technical issue, teams must **pause the match** and contact an admin.
- If a player cannot reconnect after a CS2 update, an admin must be contacted.
- Matches must be scheduled and played between **12:00 PM and 12:00 AM EST/EDT**.
- Teams must submit conflicts **1 hour before the match**.

## 6. Eligibility & Rosters

### Team Captain
- During Crossfire registration, teams will be required to designate a **team captain** via the signup form.
- The designated captain must be a player from the team's Crossfire core.
- Designated Captains may request a captain role change to be transferred anytime but may be a part of the core.

### Roster Rules
- Roster changes are allowed **until the Playoff Stage**, at which point rosters lock.
- Teams must play with at least **3 original core players** per match.
- **Maximum roster size:** 8 (5 starters, 2 substitutes, 1 coach).
- **No multi-accounting** is allowed.
- Entry fees are **non-refundable** for any reason.
- Teams failing to pay the entry fee may be removed.
- **Team Coaches are not permitted to substitute** at any point in the event.
- **All players must reside in North America.**

### Roster Lock
- All Crossfire rosters will be **locked once the Playoff Stage begins**.
- A form will be sent out for all playoff rosters to complete for the roster lock.

### Roster Changes
- Roster changes are allowed **anytime throughout the group stage**.
- Players added through a roster change **cannot be considered** a part of the core 3-5 of your original roster.

### Substitutions
- Teams may substitute a player **between matches** for any reason, provided they maintain a majority of their registered core, except in cases of a verifiable emergency.
- Substitutions **during a match or between maps** are only permitted in the event of a verifiable emergency.
- All emergencies are subject to approval at the **Admin's discretion**.

## 8. Forfeits

- Teams must have **5 players on the server within 15 minutes** of the scheduled start time.
- Failure to do so will result in a **forfeit**, scored as a **13-0 win** or **2-0 win**.
- If neither team can field 5 players, the match may be **rescheduled** or **double forfeited**.
- If no match is scheduled before the default time, the team that shows up will receive the **FFW (Forfeit Win)**.
- **Habitual forfeits** may result in bans from future Crossfire GG seasons.

---

## 9. Tech Problems & Resolutions

- Vote for a **pause** for standard timeout (up to 3 uses per team, 30 seconds each).
- Use **!pause** for serious technical issues.
- Teams must inform admins **immediately** during technical timeouts.
- Teams have **10 minutes** to resolve technical issues unless otherwise permitted by admins.
- Admins may pause matches at any time for any technical or competitive reason.

---

## 10. Exploits, Bugs, Infractions, & Penalties

- Exploiting known or unknown bugs for advantage is **prohibited**.
- **Pixel walking, invisible ledges, or glitching** is banned.
- No bomb plants in **floating positions** or **through solid objects**.
- Bombs must be **audible** to the opposing team.
- Players must **not join the enemy team** during a match.
- Boosting involving **clipping is illegal**.
- All infractions will be handled **case-by-case basis**.
- Penalties can include **forfeits** or **disqualification**.
- **Betting on your own matches** is strictly prohibited.

---

## 11. Cheating, Stream Sniping, and Other Offenses

- **Cheating of any kind** results in immediate expulsion from Crossfire GG.
- **Account sharing** during matches results in match forfeiture and expulsion.
- Players must run **FACEIT Anti-Cheat** during all matches.
- Players with **active VAC bans or platform bans** are not eligible.
- If a player is banned mid-season, **all their team's results are forfeited**.
- At **no point** should any player or coach be in a Twitch stream of the match. It doesn't matter if it's between maps or a tech pause. **Zero tolerance policy** on this.
- Use of **racial slurs** results in an **FFL**, and a second offense leads to a **tournament ban**.
