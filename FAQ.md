## FACEIT

**Q: Do all players need a FACEIT account?**  
A: Yes. All matches and servers are hosted on the FACEIT platform, therefore all players are required to have a FACEIT Account.

**Q: Is FACEIT Anti-Cheat required?**  
A: Yes. FACEIT Anti-Cheat is required for all Crossfire matches. Matches will not start if a player does not have their anti-cheat enabled.

**Q: Can I use the "Call Admin" button on FACEIT match pages?**  
A: No. Whilst there is a "Call Admin" button on all FACEIT match pages, they will not be used for official admin communication. All communication must be done through Discord.

## Streaming

**Q: Can I stream my POV of Crossfire League games?**  
A: Yes. Players are allowed to stream their POV of all Crossfire League games once they adhere to the following:
- Must have a **2 minute delay** on your stream
- Must include **@PlayCrossfire** in your stream title
- Ensure that **Store past broadcasts** and **Always Publish VODs** are enabled

## General Questions

**Q: Can I register without paying immediately?**  
A: Registration and payment details are specified in [REGISTRATION.md](REGISTRATION.md). Payment deadline is **February 23rd, 2026 @ 8:00 PM EST**.

**Q: What if we can't field 5 players at match time?**  
A: Teams must have 5 players on the server within 15 minutes of the scheduled start time. Failure to do so will result in a forfeit.

**Q: Can we change our roster after registration?**  
A: Yes, roster changes are allowed during the Group Stage. Rosters lock for the Playoff Stage.

**Q: What if a player gets banned mid-season?**  
A: If a player is banned mid-season, all team results are forfeited per the rules.

**Q: What if we're not VRS ranked?**  
A: Non-VRS teams are welcome! You'll be seeded based on ESEA League Division and W/L record.
