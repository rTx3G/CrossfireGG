## Seeding Notes

### VRS Seeding Priority

- Teams with Valve Regional Standings (VRS) ranking are seeded **first**
- Teams are ordered by their **VRS points** from the February 2nd, 2026 leaderboard
- Only teams appearing in both the Season 4 team list AND the VRS leaderboard are VRS-seeded

### Non-VRS Teams

- Remaining teams seeded based on:
  - ESEA League Division
  - Win/Loss (W/L) record
- Teams without VRS rankings are seeded randomly for now

## Group Stage Distribution

**32 Teams** divided into **8 Groups** of **4 Teams** each

### Group Assignments

Groups will be announced before Round 1 begins.

**Seeding Method:**
- Seeds 1-8 placed in separate groups (one per group)
- Seeds 9-16 distributed across groups
- Seeds 17-24 distributed across groups
- Seeds 25-32 distributed across groups

This ensures balanced groups with mixed skill levels.
